Frontend teams already learned how to split monoliths without breaking product experience. AI systems are going through a similar shift.

This talk traces the move from federated UI and composable runtime systems to a federation of AI specialists. It focuses on why production AI works better when agents have clear roles, controlled tools, workflow hooks, shared collaboration context, and policy boundaries.
