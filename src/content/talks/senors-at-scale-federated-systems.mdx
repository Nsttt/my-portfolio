A conversation with Dan Neciu and Zack Chapple about deploying federated frontends globally, the operational model behind Zephyr Cloud, and how Module Federation maps to edge delivery.

We cover Zephyr's build-to-edge workflow, reverse tree shaking, local development workflows for microfrontends, federated MCP servers for enterprise AI orchestration, and the Node.js module unloading work needed for better server-side hot reloading.
